Delicious Pizza

Description

What a delicious pepperoni pizza this is!

Follow these ingredients and directions and you'll be salivating in no time!

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Base
  • Tomato Base
  • Mozarella Pizza
  • Pepperoni
  • Chilli

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees
  2. Top the base with the tomato sauce
  3. Sprinkle the mozarella over the top of the tomate base
  4. Place the pepperoni evenly across the pizza
  5. Place the pizza in the oven for 10 mins
  6. Take it out and enjoy!!
